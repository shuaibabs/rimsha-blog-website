# **App Name**: Rimsha's Tech Blogs

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Blog Content Integration: Integrate and display blog posts from a Blogger URL, loading content dynamically via RSS feed or Blogger API to show modern cards with image, title, preview text, and 'read more' button.
- AI-Powered Blog Summarizer: Utilize an AI tool to generate concise preview summaries for blog posts, giving readers a quick overview of the content before clicking through.
- Futuristic Navigational Pages: Implement key pages including Home, Blog, AI Trends, Future of Robotics, About, and Contact, ensuring clear and intuitive navigation reflecting the app's theme.
- Advanced Blog Post Search: Provide a robust search bar functionality to allow users to easily find specific blog posts by keywords or phrases across the integrated content.
- User Engagement Features: Include a newsletter signup form to build an audience and social sharing buttons on blog posts to facilitate content distribution.
- Interactive Homepage & UI: Design a captivating hero section with an animated headline ('Exploring the Future of Artificial Intelligence') and an AI-inspired animated background, along with organized sections for key content categories.
- Contact and About Section: Create a dedicated section showcasing COER University, Roorkee student (cu240251326@coeruniversity.ac.in), information, establishing authenticity and academic background.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark mode with primary color: a vibrant electric blue (#1F6BF0) to evoke innovation and technology. This hue brings energy, contrasting subtly with darker backgrounds.
- Background color: A very dark, desaturated blue (#16191C), providing a deep, immersive foundation for the neon accents, aligning with an advanced AI lab aesthetic.
- Accent color: A striking cyan (#6BE2F8) used for calls to action, highlights, and interactive elements. Its luminous quality enhances the futuristic and holographic feel of the UI.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), chosen for its computerized and techy aesthetic, perfectly fitting the AI-inspired theme. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), for excellent readability and a modern, objective feel in blog posts and longer text sections.
- Use sleek, geometric, and line-based icons with subtle glowing effects. Visual elements like circuit patterns, digital particles, and glowing grids should be integrated throughout to reinforce the futuristic AI and robotics theme.
- Implement a large, impactful hero section on the homepage, modern card layouts for blog posts with responsive design. Ensure smooth content flow and clear hierarchical arrangement across all pages, resembling a sci-fi technology dashboard.
- Incorporate smooth transitions, floating UI elements, glowing hover effects on cards, and subtle animated gradients. The background should feature futuristic animated particles, neural network visualizations, or subtle grid motion. Include parallax scrolling sections and a futuristic loading animation to enhance user engagement and visual appeal.
